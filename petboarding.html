Bring your Dogs & Cats to vacation with us at our Acreage in Borden, SK.

Dogs

We do not discriminate based on your dog's breed, but we like to get to know them a little before they come to stay in our home. Please ask to visit with your dog if you have never been here before! We happily accept dogs who have never been away from home before and will crate train those that have never been in a crate. Dogs are exercised in one of the five HUGE fenced areas and have plenty of smells, sights, sounds, and gopher holes to investigate. In the summer, there are paddling pools, toys, shaded treed areas, and two decks so your dog can play, have fun, and then rest to their heart's content! Not every dog is dog-friendly, and that's okay; they can still come here. They also get lots of playtime, either on their own or with us playing frisbee, ball, etc. In the winter, we carve out trails for the dogs to explore and encourage them to play ball, dig in the snow, and even prune the trees for us. However, they are also free to hang out inside if they don't like the cold. We cater to all sizes and ages - puppies and seniors are welcome. Our exercise areas are Chihuahua and Great Dane approved! All dogs sleep in their comfy crates indoors at night in our home, which is smoke-free and air-conditioned.

Cats

The Kitty Condos were purpose-built for cats. They enjoy their own 6x4 indoor air-conditioned/heated cat room and their own 5x10 outside "Catio" (a patio for cats!). We are proud to say that these are the biggest indoor individual cat rooms in Saskatchewan and the only ones with adjoining individual outside cat runs! They are not metal vet cages stacked two or three high; they are proper rooms with actual doors, just what your cat is used to! Just provide your cat's food and a bed, and we'll supply the rest! Each indoor room comes complete with a cat tree, scratch posts, a cat door to the outside, a litter box, carpet in winter, cool flooring in summer, and lots of toys. You are very welcome to provide your own things too. Outside, they are safe and enclosed but can still watch the birds, squirrels, and chase leaves. We have about four squirrels that we feed, and they delight in taunting the cats! We only have four rooms, and they book up fast and well in advance as we have many repeat clients.

Pricing

Cats

One cat is $15 per day. Two cats are $20 per day, and three cats are $25 per day. Cats must be from same family to share.
